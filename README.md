# Ayush Enterprise

Ayush Enterprise is a premium gym equipment supplier offering high-quality fitness equipment for home and commercial gyms. This website showcases our wide range of products including dumbbells, treadmills, benches, barbells, resistance bands, and accessories. The site is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices.

Founded in 2015, Ayush Enterprise has been serving fitness enthusiasts and commercial gyms with premium quality equipment and excellent after-sales support.

## Features

- Product catalog with category filtering
- WhatsApp integration for instant customer support
- Google Maps integration showing store location
- Responsive design for all devices
- Contact form for inquiries
- About section with company information
